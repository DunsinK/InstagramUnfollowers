Instagram Unfollowers

Ever wonder who’s unfollowed you on Instagram?

This app makes it easy to find out — safely and securely. By using the official Instagram data download feature, you can upload your followers and following files to instantly see who isn’t following you back. No scraping, no bots, and no risk of getting banned — your information stays completely private on your device.

How to Download Your Instagram Data

Read the instructions or Watch the quick tutorial to learn how to download the files needed for this tool.

  • Open the Instagram app or website and go to Settings.
  • Navigate to Privacy and Security (or Your Activity on mobile).
  • Select Download Your Information.
  • Request your data, then check your email for a download link from Instagram.
  • Once you receive the data, extract the files and locate following.html and followers_1.html.
  • Drag and drop these two files into the fields below to see who has unfollowed you.
